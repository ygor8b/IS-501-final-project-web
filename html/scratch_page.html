My Spanish Journey

Over the past year, I’ve been dedicated to learning Spanish — and it’s been one of the most rewarding experiences of my life. It started as a challenge, but turned into a passion that deepened during my time in Mexico. That experience not only helped me improve my fluency, but also gave me new appreciation for culture, connection, and communication.

Now fluent in three languages — Portuguese, English, and Spanish — I’ve experienced firsthand how learning a new language strengthens the brain and opens new doors professionally and personally. Being multilingual allows me to connect with more people, adapt faster in different environments, and broaden my opportunities both academically and in the workplace.

Recent data shows a clear correlation between skill development and higher wages — especially in today's global economy where communication and adaptability are in high demand. Language learning is not just about speaking differently; it's about thinking differently, navigating new perspectives, and adding value in multicultural settings. That’s why I continue to invest in learning: every new skill I build — especially languages — increases my potential to contribute, earn, and grow.
